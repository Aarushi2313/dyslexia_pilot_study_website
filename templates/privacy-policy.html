Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to support our research on dyslexia detection. This includes:

  • Personal Account Data: Name, email address, and encrypted passwords.
  • Demographic Data: Age, gender, native language, education level, and dyslexia status (if known).
  • Performance Data: Audio recordings of reading tasks, typing speed metrics, comprehension quiz answers, and handwriting samples.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data collected is used strictly for:

  • Training and validating Natural Language Processing (NLP) models for dyslexia identification.
  • Improving the accuracy of our screening tools.
  • Academic research and statistical analysis.

Your personally identifiable information (PII) is kept separate from research data wherever possible.

3. Data Protection

We implement robust security measures to protect your data. This includes encryption of passwords, secure server storage, and restricted access to raw data. Audio recordings and sensitive inputs are handled with strict confidentiality.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. De-identified, aggregated research data may be shared in academic publications or with research partners. Any such data will not contain information that could identify you personally.

5. Children's Privacy

This platform is intended for use by students, including minors, with parental or guardian consent. We take special care to protect the privacy of children under in accordance with applicable laws (e.g., DPDP, POCSO).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, to correct any inaccuracies, or to request the deletion of your account and data. Please contact us to exercise these rights.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@dyslexiastudy.com.
